Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a profile?

After signing up, tap "Edit Profile" to add your name, title, company, bio, and profile photo. Your profile card updates in real time.

How do I switch between profile modes?

On the Profile tab, tap the mode selector to switch between Professional, Personal, Creative, Student, and Freelancer. Each mode can have its own theme color.

How do I share my profile?

Tap the QR code icon or the share button on your profile. Others can scan your QR code or receive your profile link directly.

How do I add social links?

Go to your profile and tap "Social Links". You can add links for various platforms including LinkedIn, Twitter, Instagram, GitHub, and more.

How do I delete my account?

Go to Settings and tap "Delete Account". This will permanently remove all your profile data and authentication credentials from our systems.

Is my data secure?

Yes. Profyl uses Firebase Authentication for secure sign-in and Firebase Firestore for data storage. Your profile data is only accessible to you. Read our Privacy Policy for more details.
